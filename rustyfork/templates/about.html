About The Rusty Fork

The Rusty Fork is a family-owned and operated bistro that opened in 2020. Our mission is to provide our guests with a warm and friendly atmosphere, where they can enjoy quality food and drinks at reasonable prices.

We source our ingredients from local farmers and suppliers, who share our passion for sustainability and freshness. We also support our community by hosting events, fundraisers, and live music nights.

Our team is composed of experienced and talented chefs, servers, and bartenders, who are always ready to make your visit memorable.

Come and join us at The Rusty Fork, where you'll feel like home!
